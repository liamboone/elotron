    The Arcs

    Each arc on the outside of the circle reflects the total number of points a given player has scored against all opponents (i.e., 'taken' points).

    The colors of each arc are randomly assigned, so they might change upon page reloads.

    The Chords

    Each chord propogating from each arc reflects the relationship between two players.

    The chord will be colored to indicate which player has scored more points than the other.

    The relative sizes of the two ends of a chord also indicate the numbers of points scored against the other player.

    Interact

    Mouse over any player's outer arc to get detailed info on their activity and stats. Mouse over any chord to get detailed info on the interaction between the two players that the chord connects.
